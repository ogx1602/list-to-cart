List-to-Cart

Any list, anywhere — straight into your Walmart cart. Rolling out EOM.
This document is private and confidential. For internal use only.
▶ See Phase 1 Demo

Customers already have lists.
Let’s meet them there.

Today, ~80% of American households shop with a grocery list — often to avoid the “forgotten item.” Customers need a seamless way to map list items to shoppable products, personalized to them, with an easy path to add them to cart.

From a casual shopping list on Apple Notes to a personalized,
shoppable basket — without leaving the list.
Works with the lists customers already use
 
List-to-Cart meets customers in the tools they already use to plan their shop. By owning the list-to-basket moment, we capture intent earlier, reduce the friction of manual search, and deepen a strategic bet with Apple — while keeping the conversion, the relationship, and the long-term value inside the Walmart ecosystem.
 
What’s included in Phase 1
 
 
 
 Apple Notes entry point — parse a grocery list and map each item to a shoppable Walmart product
 
 Bespoke grocery CX — tuned to the nuances of grocery, extensible to BTS and holiday shopping
 
 Personalized matching — items resolved to the right product, personalized to the customer
 
 One-tap basket building — straight from list to cart, no manual search
Success is measured by ATC and cart ingresses from List, with a qualitative read on the strategic Apple relationship this launch accrues to.
 
Phase 1 - Launch Steps
 
 
Discoverability: Awareness lives on the Walmart ecosystem rather than the Apple side, so we’re exploring ways to intercept the user journey:
 
–  Placement in Cart, My Items, and Home
–  Marketing and email comms to drive first use
 
Timeline: EOM Launch in production
 
What’s Next
 
 
Phase 2
New input sources & modalities: handwritten notes, screenshots, Siri VUI, Sparky overlay, and receipt upload (synergy with B2B, leveraging each other’s work).
Phase 3
Upsell, cross-sell, bundles, and recipes built on top of the matched basket.
